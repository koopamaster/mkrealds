MARIO KART REAL DS

Mario Kart Real DS is one port of Mario Kart Live:Home Circuit on the Ds

Actually, one demo is available, check the "Dowload & Updates" Button
This hack contains 4 differents tracks :
-One classic room: Room-1 , looks like one 8
-Mario Bros race: World 1-1 , one 8bit designed track
-One track with a really big road for speed : PC Room
-One room in the night ! Boos are coming back!: Ghosted Room -One PlaceHolder
-And one Award Ceremony (Unplayable) track
