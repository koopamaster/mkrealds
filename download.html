Download and releases:

Now, there's only a demo available.

Due to NitroWav ASM Hack, wo versions of demo are downloadable, one stable and one nightly.
The stable doesn't have music, and the nightly has full music, from famous Mario games.
If you want playing on real hardware, Nintendo DS and DS Lite, the stable one is really recommended!
For all others, like Twilight Menu++ or PC emulator, just use the nightly.
