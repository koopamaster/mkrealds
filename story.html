Story of MKRealDs

THE STORY OF THIS HACK !!!
All began the first day that Nintendo announced this Mario Kart 9 (They posted a trailer)
So I started the first track (Room 1) ...
After some weeks : The 9 October, I finished all tracks (Room 1 , Pc Room , World 1-1 and Ghosted Room (It's my favourite one! ^^ )). But sadly , I travelled and my connectivity was almost dead during one week, That's why I didn't released all tracks BEFORE NINTENDO !!! =0 ...
I have felt very sad ,I lose, Nintendo has won ! = (
After one complete night of thinking about this, I finally decided to make my hack more complete (all sounds, all graphs, etc..)
The 14 November, Almost all stuff was finished, CaptainLuigi & TutoBrico did some beta testing, and they reported some important bugs , also they gave me some good ideas.
And we started to use NitroWaves (ASM Hack by TheGameratorT)
Also, I decided to make one full custom menu, all tracks' maps, custom item-boxes and a lot of other thing !
In December, I did one little pause, for continuing my other projects, and I wanted to make one little gifts for Christmas.
I made one special Christmas themed track : watch it !!!
So, in 2021 , I made the two last tracks :
-Award ceremony track
-One placeholder
All stuff was completed!!
I made the official hack's trailer : https://youtu.be/bW9eRNPF5W0
And one tutorial : https://youtu.be/uYjOlfsLsns
Uh! So , it's all XD

This is only one demo, One full version will be released soon (probably in next months?! XD)
